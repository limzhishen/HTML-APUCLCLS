HOT Questions

You only need to fill out the form and conduct an interview at the designated location, our address is listed on the Contact Us page of the webpage.

You can check it out by going to the Tour Events section of the home page, which lists upcoming events. If you want to know more information, you can go to the event's webpage to view.

The activity cycle of the Chinese Language Society is four days per month, one day per week.

The Chinese Language Society generally organizes activities on traditional Chinese festivals, such as: New Year, Mid-Autumn Festival, Dragon Boat Festival, Winter Solstice, etc.

There are five clubs in the Chinese Club: the Chinese Orchestra, the Dance Club, the Debating Club, the Media Club and the Diabolo Club.

The cost you need to prepare is to purchase a RM35 team shirt when you join the group for the first time, and a monthly fee of RM8 per month.
