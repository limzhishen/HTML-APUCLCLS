Upcoming Event

 

The wind is blowing! What's blowing? The scent of spring plum blossoms, the thumping of gongs and drums... It also brings our joyous Chinese New Year celebration, "Memory of the Beginning"!
The symbolic meaning of "Yi-Kai" is the remembrance of old times and the departure of new journeys. We heard that the taste of the year seems to be fading little by little, although things will fade away, but goodness will not.
Let's look back on a bygone era and embrace an old and simple New Year; let's continue to look forward to the future. In these epidemic times, it is important to cherish and be with each other, and to give local and foreign students the opportunity to relive and experience what New Year is and what it has to offer. This is the aim of this event.
