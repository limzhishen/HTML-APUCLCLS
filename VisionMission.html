VISION MISSION

Vision

To become a leading organization that represents Chinese culture and heritage, to connect Chinese people and communities worldwide, and to serve as a platform for innovation, creativity, and collaboration.

Mission

To promote Chinese culture, language, and traditions, to foster cross-cultural exchange and understanding between Chinese and non-Chinese people, and to provide a supportive community for Chinese students or immigrants.

Achievement

Organizing cultural events such as Chinese New Year celebrations, traditional festivals, or language and calligraphy workshops; participating in community service and volunteer projects that benefit Chinese or other minority groups; facilitating networking and career development opportunities for Chinese professionals or students; or promoting cross-cultural understanding and dialogue through intercultural events or collaborations with other organizations
